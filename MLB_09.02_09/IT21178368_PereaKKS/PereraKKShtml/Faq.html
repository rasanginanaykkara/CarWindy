FAQs

What are the brands do you offer?

We offer all brands availabe in Sri Lanka


What type of driver's license do I need to drive?

You will need a valid Local Driving Permit issued by the Department of Motor Traffic in Sri Lanka.


Can I specify the grade and color of the car?

No. Due to availability, a specific model including but not limited to vehicle model year, grade, and color cannot be specified. You can choose vehicle according to no of passengers, fuel type, car type, luggage space and transmission of the car.


Can I make changes to my reservation?

Yes. The reservation number issued when you made the reservation and the email address you registered at the time of reservation are required to make changes to your reservation.


Can I return the car to a different rental station?

Yes. We provide a one-way service that allows return of the vehicle to a different rental station.


Can I pay by credit card?

Yes. Credit cards are accepted.
